Tribute to Pepe the Frog

Pepe the Frog

Timeline

  • 2005 - Artist Matt Furie created the comic series Boy’s Club, which stars the teenage monster characters Pepe, Brett, Andy and Landwolf
  • 2008 - A comic in which Pepe pulls his pants down to his ankles in order to urinate was popularized on 4chan’s /b/, (random) board, along with the expression “Feels good man”
  • 2009 - An edited version featuring a distraught-looking Pepe with the caption “Feels bad man” began circulating as a reaction image on 4chan and the Body Building Forums
  • 2014
    • June 13th - PepeTheFrogBlog Tumblr blog was launched
    • October 25th - /r/pepethefrog subreddit was launched for content featuring the frog character
    • December 7th - a Facebook page for “Pepe the Frog” was created
  • 2015 - A phenomenon began on 4chan where users would declare certain variants as rare, known as a "rare Pepe". These images, sometimes as physical paintings, were put up for sale and auction on eBay and posted in listings on Craigslist.
  • 2016 - Associations of Pepe with Donald Trump's campaign, white nationalism, and the alt-right were described by various news organizations. The Anti-Defamation League included Pepe in its hate symbol database but noted that most instances of Pepe were not used in a hate-related context
